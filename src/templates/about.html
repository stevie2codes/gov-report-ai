About Gov-Report-AI

Transforming government data into actionable insights through AI-powered report generation

Project Overview

Gov-Report-AI is a micro-SaaS platform designed to streamline government report generation. Our mission is to transform raw CSV/XLSX data into professional, compliance-ready reports that match the exact style of traditional government documents while adding speed, accessibility, and AI-driven insights.

Built specifically for city, county, and state agencies, this tool addresses the unique needs of government reporting while maintaining the professional standards required in public sector work.
